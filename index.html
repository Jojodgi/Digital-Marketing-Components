Video Definition

Video marketing is mainly based on video websites as the core network platform, content-centric and creative-oriented, and uses well-planned video content to achieve product marketing and brand communication purposes.
The combination of "video" and "internet" gives this innovative marketing form the advantages of both: it has some of the characteristics of TV short films, such as strong infection, diverse forms and content, wanton creativity, etc., but at the same time it has Internet marketing Advantage.
Because of the rapid development of the Internet, video marketing has brought a new platform for interactive marketing. With the development of the Internet and the rise of video websites, video marketing has become more and more valued by many brand companies and has become an important part of online marketing.

Future direction

Content Native
The difference between content-native advertising and embedded advertising is that it no longer simply embeds the brand, but instead produces content around the product, making the content an advertisement for the product.
Technology Driven
In Internet content information platforms, the importance of technology (algorithms) continues to increase. Specifically, the algorithmic leadership can truly and effectively mine user needs, making the platform content recommendation more and more able to meet user needs.
User Initiative
User active behavior monitoring technology, adding the value of user active behavior brought by mobile video advertising to the evaluation system, and actively recording the user's active behavior generated by this advertisement, how to achieve true commercial marketing on short video platforms.
