We chose this topic for three reasons：

 

Professional related：

As a student studying communications, a model like video marketing is covered by our major. growing especially popular：

Secondly, by understanding this topic, we found that videos can help increase user awareness of their products or services. For people, from a brand perspective, people prefer video content over email, newsletters, social pictures, social videos, blog posts and downloadable content (such as Pdf).
